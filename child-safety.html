Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Mae Fah Luang University

Summary: StudySync strictly prohibits any content or behavior that exploits or endangers minors. Users can report safety concerns in-app or by contacting us directly. We comply with all applicable child safety laws and cooperate with relevant authorities.
01

Our Commitment

StudySync is committed to providing a safe platform for all users. We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activity that exploits, abuses, or endangers children in any way, including child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

StudySync is designed exclusively for university students aged 18 and older. We take child safety obligations seriously and maintain zero tolerance for any violation of these standards.

02

Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on StudySync:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Any content that sexualizes or endangers children
  • Sharing personal information of minors without consent
  • Any communication intended to gain inappropriate access to a minor

Accounts found violating these rules are permanently banned and reported to relevant authorities without exception.

03

Reporting Mechanisms

StudySync provides in-app reporting tools that allow users to report any child safety concern directly. All reports are reviewed promptly and appropriate action is taken.

  • In-app report button available on all user profiles and content
  • Reports are reviewed within 24 to 48 hours
  • Confirmed violations result in immediate account suspension and permanent ban
  • Severe cases are reported to national and regional authorities
04

Compliance

StudySync complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. We report confirmed cases of CSAM to relevant national and regional authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.

We maintain internal procedures to detect, report, and respond to child safety violations, and regularly review these practices to ensure continued compliance.

05

06

Policy Updates

We may update these standards periodically to reflect changes in law, platform features, or best practices. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice. Continued use of StudySync constitutes acceptance of the most current version of this policy.
